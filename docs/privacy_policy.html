Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

CitizenPrep ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application developed by Labsby to help users study for the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) civics test. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

CitizenPrep does not require you to create an account. We do not collect, store, or transmit your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information to our servers. All study progress, quiz history, and settings are stored locally on your device only.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is saved on your device and never leaves it:

  • Quiz scores and history
  • Flashcard progress (known / review status)
  • XP, level, streaks, and achievements
  • App settings (language, test version, senior mode, dark mode)
  • Selected state and congressional district (for state-specific questions)
  • Ad-removal purchase status

This data is stored using the Hive embedded database on your device. It is removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

CitizenPrep shows advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • General location (country/region level, derived from IP address)
  • App usage data for ad targeting and measurement

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy . You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

Ads are never shown while you are actively answering a question. Users who purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase will no longer see banner or interstitial advertisements.

4. In-App Purchases

CitizenPrep offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase for $4.99 processed through Google Play. We do not handle or store your payment information. All payment processing is managed by Google in accordance with their Terms of Service .

5. Internet Access

The app connects to the internet for two purposes:

  • State officials data: CitizenPrep periodically fetches a publicly available JSON file hosted on GitHub to keep U.S. Senator, Representative, Governor, and state capital information current. No user data is sent in this request.
  • Advertisements: Ad content is fetched from Google AdMob servers as described in Section 3.

6. Children's Privacy

CitizenPrep is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
